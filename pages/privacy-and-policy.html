Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to BootZip Creator, a free and open-source application developed by Jahangir Alam. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information is used to provide the app's functionality and how we protect your privacy.

Open Source

BootZip Creator is a fully open-source application. We believe in transparency and community collaboration. You can view the source code, contribute to the project, or report issues on our GitHub repository:

https://github.com/JahangirAlamSuvro/Boot-Zip-Creator.git

Information We Collect and Use

BootZip Creator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from its users. The app is designed to work entirely offline on your device, except for checking for updates.

The app uses the following libraries for its core functionality:

  • AndroidX AppCompat, Material Components
  • Activity, ConstraintLayout
  • JUnit and Espresso for testing
  • Media3 ExoPlayer for video processing
  • Mobile FFmpeg AAR for video conversion
  • Smart Exception Java, Glide, SwipeRefreshLayout
  • In-app update library

These libraries are used solely for app functionality and do not send your personal data to us or any third party.

Permissions Used

BootZip Creator requires the following permissions to function correctly:

  • INTERNET: Required for checking for and downloading app updates.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To send you notifications about app updates or important alerts.

These permissions are only used for the stated purposes. No data is collected from your device without your consent.

How We Use Information

The app accesses your device storage only to read and process the video files you select to generate a bootanimation.zip file. No videos, personal data, or usage information are ever sent outside of your device.

Third-Party Services

BootZip Creator does not use any third-party services for advertising, analytics, or monetization. The app is completely ad-free.

Your Rights

  • You have full control over the video files you select within the app.
  • You can manage app permissions at any time through your device settings.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to remove it and its data from your device.
