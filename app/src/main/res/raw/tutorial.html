Step 1: Select Your Video

Click the Select Video button and choose your favorite boot animation video from your device.

Step 2: Choose Looping Part

Use the range slider to select the looping portion of the video for your boot animation.

Tap the Play button to preview only the selected part in a loop.
To play the entire video instead, simply long press the Play button.
